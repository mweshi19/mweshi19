About Kydeva Sacco

Our Mission

To empower our members with financial solutions that enhance their quality of life and contribute to the economic development of our community.

What is a SACCO?

A Savings and Credit Cooperative Society (SACCO) is a member-owned financial institution that offers savings and credit services to its members. SACCOs are typically established by a group of people with a common interest or goal, such as farmers, workers, or small business owners, who pool their resources and provide financial support to each other. SACCOs operate on the principles of cooperation, self-help, and mutual benefit. They offer a range of financial services, including savings accounts, loans, and investment opportunities, to help members achieve their financial goals.

Why Choose Kydeva Sacco?

  • Affordable Loans: We offer competitive interest rates on loans to help our members grow their businesses and improve their livelihoods.
  • Financial Education: We provide financial literacy programs to help our members make informed financial decisions.
  • Community Focus: We are committed to the development and prosperity of our community.
